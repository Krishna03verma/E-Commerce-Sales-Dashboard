## 📌 Overview

The E-Commerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze business performance across sales, profit, orders, product categories, payment methods, and regional markets.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key business metrics, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance, identify top-selling products, analyze customer purchasing behavior, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

E-commerce companies generate large volumes of sales transactions across multiple product categories, regions, and payment channels.

Without proper analysis, businesses may struggle to:

- Monitor revenue and profitability.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Understand customer payment preferences.
- Evaluate category-wise performance.
- Track regional sales performance.
- Make informed business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that:

- Tracks overall sales performance.
- Monitors revenue and profit trends.
- Identifies top-selling products.
- Analyzes category-wise sales contribution.
- Evaluates customer payment preferences.
- Supports strategic business decision-making.
